<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Battery Thermal Management System (BTMS) market comprises two types: Type I and Type II. Type I includes passive thermal management solutions, utilizing materials that maintain optimal battery temperatures without active components, enhancing efficiency and reducing costs. Type II encompasses active thermal management systems, which use mechanical systems like pumps or fans to regulate temperature, providing more precise control under varying conditions. Both types aim to extend battery life, improve performance, and ensure safety in electric vehicles.</p></p>
