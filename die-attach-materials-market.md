<p><strong>The Die-Attach Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die Attach Paste</li><li>Die Attach Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The die-attach materials market consists primarily of die attach paste and die attach wire. Die attach paste is a viscous conductive adhesive used to bond semiconductor chips to substrate materials, ensuring thermal and electrical conductivity while providing mechanical stability. Die attach wire, on the other hand, involves bonding wire used for interconnecting the die to external circuitry, ensuring reliable electrical connections. Both types are essential for encapsulating and packaging electronic components, enhancing performance in various applications.</p></p>
