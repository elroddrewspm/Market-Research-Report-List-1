<p><strong>The Dental Healing Cap Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Healing Cap</li><li>Angled Healing Cap</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental healing cap market consists of two main types: straight healing caps and angled healing caps. Straight healing caps are designed to fit directly onto implants, providing a stable platform for healing and tissue integration. They ensure a straightforward alignment for prosthetic attachments. In contrast, angled healing caps are utilized when a specific angle is required to achieve optimal aesthetics and function, accommodating various implant placements. Both types play crucial roles in the dental implant recovery process, promoting successful osseointegration.</p></p>
