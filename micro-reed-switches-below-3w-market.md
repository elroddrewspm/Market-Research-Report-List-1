<p><p>Micro reed switches are highly sensitive devices designed for low-power applications, typically handling loads below 3W. The market is divided into two main types: Normally Open (NO) and Normally Closed (NC) switches. Normally Open switches close the circuit only when activated by a magnetic field, while Normally Closed switches maintain the circuit until a magnetic field interrupts it. These switches are widely used in security systems, automotive applications, and various consumer electronics, providing reliable and efficient performance in compact designs.</p></p>
