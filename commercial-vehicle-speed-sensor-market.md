<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial vehicle speed sensor market comprises two primary types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors use magnetic fields to detect the speed of rotating components, delivering accurate and reliable performance in various conditions. Magnetic Electric sensors, on the other hand, utilize electromagnetic principles to measure speed by generating electrical signals from magnetic field changes. Both types are essential for modern vehicle systems, enhancing safety and vehicle performance by providing critical speed data for diagnostics and control functions.</p></p>
