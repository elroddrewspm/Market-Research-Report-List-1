<p><p>The fiber cable termination market encompasses various products, predominantly fiber optic pigtails and fanout kits. Fiber optic pigtails are short lengths of fiber designed to connect a fiber optic cable to a connector, facilitating efficient linking in networks. Fanout kits, on the other hand, are used to split a single fiber into multiple fibers, allowing for greater distribution of connections. Both are essential for ensuring reliable and high-performance fiber optic communication in network infrastructure.</p></p>
