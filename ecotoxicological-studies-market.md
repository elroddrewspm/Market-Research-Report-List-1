<p><p>The ecotoxicological studies market is segmented into various types that focus on different ecosystems. Terrestrial ecotoxicology assesses the effects on land-dwelling organisms, including plants and soil invertebrates. Avian ecotoxicology focuses on birds and their exposure to toxic substances, and pollinator testing investigates the implications of pollutants on bees and other pollinators, all vital for ecosystem health and biodiversity.</p></p>
