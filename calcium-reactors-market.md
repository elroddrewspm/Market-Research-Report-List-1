<p><p>Calcium reactors are essential in reef aquariums for maintaining optimal calcium and alkalinity levels. The market features two main types: single chamber and dual chamber structures. Single chamber reactors have a simpler design, where all processes occur in one compartment, making them easy to use but potentially less efficient. In contrast, dual chamber reactors separate the processes of calcium dissolution and carbon dioxide introduction, enhancing efficiency and stability in calcium addition, making them suitable for larger or more demanding systems.</p></p>
