In contrast, AOI Testing Equipment inspects the visual quality and accuracy of display components by identifying defects in manufacturing through high-resolution imaging. Both types are essential for ensuring product quality, enhancing performance, and minimizing defects in the production of flat-panel displays.</p></p>
