<p><p>The adhesives used in LED packaging are categorized into three main types: liquid, solid, and paste. Liquid adhesives are typically used for their ease of application and ability to fill gaps, offering flexibility in bonding. Solid adhesives, often in the form of pellets or films, provide strong, durable bonds and are ideal for high-temperature applications. Paste adhesives maintain viscosity during application, ensuring controlled dispensing and positioning, making them suitable for intricate designs and precise assembly in LED packaging.</p></p>
