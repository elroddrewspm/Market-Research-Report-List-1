<p><p>The Uroflowmetry System market is segmented based on various urinary conditions. BPH refers to benign prostatic hyperplasia, causing urinary flow obstruction. Spontaneous urinary incontinence is the unexpected loss of urine, while stress incontinence occurs during physical activity. Bladder neuromuscular dysfunction affects bladder control, and bladder neck obstruction impedes urine flow. Lastly, post-traumatic urethral stricture results from injury, causing narrowing of the urethra. Each condition necessitates uroflowmetry for accurate diagnosis and management.</p></p>
