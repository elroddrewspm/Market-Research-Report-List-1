<p><strong>The ENT Endoscope Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid Endoscopes</li><li>Flexible Endoscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ENT endoscope market comprises two primary types: rigid endoscopes and flexible endoscopes. Rigid endoscopes feature a fixed design that provides precision and stability, making them ideal for surgical procedures and examinations that require a clear field of view, such as sinus surgeries. In contrast, flexible endoscopes are designed with a pliable body, allowing them to navigate complex anatomical structures, making them suitable for diagnostics and minimally invasive procedures in the ear, nose, and throat areas.</p></p>
