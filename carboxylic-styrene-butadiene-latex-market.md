<p><ul><li>Emulsion Polymerization</li><li>Solution Polymerization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carboxylic Styrene Butadiene Latex market comprises two primary types: emulsion polymerization and solution polymerization. Emulsion polymerization involves dispersing monomers in water, leading to the formation of latex with high molecular weight and uniform properties, making it suitable for coatings and adhesives. In contrast, solution polymerization uses organic solvents to dissolve monomers, producing latex with different characteristics, often enhancing solubility and compatibility in various applications. Both methods contribute to diverse applications in industries like automotive, construction, and textiles.</p></p>
