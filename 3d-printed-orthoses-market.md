<p><p>The 3D printed orthoses market is segmented into three main types: upper-limb orthoses, lower-limb orthoses, and spinal orthoses. Upper-limb orthoses are designed to support and immobilize the arms, wrists, and hands, often used for conditions like fractures or post-surgery recovery. Lower-limb orthoses focus on the legs and ankles, aiding mobility and stability for issues like injuries or deformities. Spinal orthoses provide support to the back and neck, addressing spinal conditions and ensuring proper alignment, enhancing patient comfort and rehabilitation.</p></p>
