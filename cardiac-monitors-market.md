<p><p>The cardiac monitors market encompasses various types, including Holter monitors, event monitors, mobile cardiac telemetry, and other devices. Holter monitors provide continuous recording of heart activity over 24-48 hours, ideal for detecting irregularities. Event monitors allow patients to manually record heart activity when experiencing symptoms, typically used for longer durations. Mobile cardiac telemetry provides real-time heart monitoring via cellular technology, enhancing patient convenience. Other types may include implantable monitors and traditional ECG devices, catering to diverse cardiac monitoring needs.</p></p>
