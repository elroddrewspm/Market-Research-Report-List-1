Discontinuous screen changers operate in a batch mode, where production halts for screen replacement, suitable for low-volume applications. In contrast, continuous screen changers allow for uninterrupted operation, enabling real-time screen replacement without stopping the extruder, making them ideal for high-volume operations. Both types enhance product quality and efficiency while minimizing downtime, catering to different production needs in the extrusion industry.</p></p>
