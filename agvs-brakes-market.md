<p><p>The Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) brakes market encompasses several types, including Permanent Magnet Power Off Brakes, Spring Applied Power Off Brakes, and others. Permanent Magnet Power Off Brakes utilize magnetic forces to engage braking when power is cut, ensuring safety. Spring Applied Power Off Brakes rely on mechanical springs to engage brakes automatically when power is lost. Other brake technologies may include hydraulic and pneumatic systems, each offering varying levels of responsiveness and applications in different AGV types.</p></p>
