<p><p>A wireless bridge is a networking device that connects two or more local area networks (LANs) using wireless technology. It facilitates data communication and Internet connectivity between remote locations without the need for physical cables, enhancing flexibility and reducing installation costs. Wireless bridges are commonly used in various applications, including offices, campuses, and industrial settings where cabling might be impractical.</p><p>The Wireless Bridge Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.4% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for high-speed internet, the expansion of smart cities, and the advent of Internet of Things (IoT) applications. Organizations are adopting wireless solutions due to their scalability and ease of deployment. Furthermore, the rise in remote work and online communication has spurred investments in reliable wireless infrastructure.</p><p>Latest trends in the market include the integration of advanced technologies such as mesh networking and improved security protocols to enhance performance and reliability.
