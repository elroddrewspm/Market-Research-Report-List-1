<p><p>Fluorocarbon films encompass materials like PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride), and PEVE (Polyethylene-Vinyl Acetate). PTFE films are known for their exceptional chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making them suitable for various industrial applications. PVDF films offer excellent weather resistance and are often used in outdoor applications, including roofing and coatings. PEVE combines properties of polyethylene and vinyl, providing versatility and enhanced adhesion. Together, these materials serve diverse markets, including aerospace, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
