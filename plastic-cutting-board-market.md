<p><ul><li>High Density Polyethylene</li><li>Low Density Polyethylene</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic cutting board market primarily features two types: High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE boards are known for their durability and resistance to deep cuts, making them ideal for heavy-duty kitchen use. They are easier to clean and maintain, resisting bacteria. In contrast, LDPE boards are softer and more flexible, suitable for light-duty tasks. They are less durable but offer easy handling and are typically used for lighter food preparation tasks. Both types cater to various consumer needs.</p></p>
