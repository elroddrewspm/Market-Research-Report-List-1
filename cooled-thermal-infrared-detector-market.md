<p><ul><li>Vanadium Oxide (VOx)</li><li>Amorphous Silicon (A-Si)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cooled thermal infrared detector market features two primary types: Vanadium Oxide (VOx) and Amorphous Silicon (A-Si). VOx detectors utilize vanadium oxide as the sensing material, known for high sensitivity and efficiency in low-temperature applications. They are favored in military and industrial uses. In contrast, Amorphous Silicon detectors are cost-effective and suitable for a range of applications, including surveillance and automotive systems. Both types serve essential roles in advancing infrared technology across diverse sectors.</p></p>
