<p><ul><li>Monocrystalline Sapphire</li><li>Trigonal System Sapphire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic sapphire market primarily includes two types: monocrystalline sapphire and trigonal system sapphire. Monocrystalline sapphire is a single crystal structure known for its superior optical clarity, making it ideal for applications in electronics and optics. In contrast, trigonal system sapphire, characterized by its unique crystal symmetry, offers distinct properties suited for specialized applications, such as in lasers and high-temperature environments. Both types are increasingly used in various industries, driven by advancements in manufacturing technologies and growing demand for durable materials.</p></p>
