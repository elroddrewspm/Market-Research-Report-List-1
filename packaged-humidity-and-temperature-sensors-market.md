<p><p>Packaged humidity and temperature sensors are essential for monitoring environmental conditions. The capacitive type operates based on the change in electrical capacitance due to humidity variations, offering high accuracy and stability. Resistive types rely on changes in electrical resistance as moisture levels fluctuate, providing a simpler and often more cost-effective solution. Other market types might include thermal, optical, or digital sensors, each employing unique mechanisms to measure humidity and temperature, catering to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
