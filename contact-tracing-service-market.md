<p><p>Contact tracing services primarily utilize three market types: Bluetooth, GPS, and other technologies. Bluetooth-based tracing employs proximity sensors to identify close contacts without disclosing location, ensuring privacy. GPS-based solutions track locations over time, providing a broader view of exposure but raising privacy concerns. Other methods may include mobile app integrations, QR codes, or manual tracking, each offering unique benefits and challenges. Together, these technologies play a crucial role in managing public health responses during outbreaks.</p></p>
