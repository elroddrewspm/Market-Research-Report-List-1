Single-pump machines use one pump for blood circulation, making them simpler and cost-effective, suitable for less complicated treatments. In contrast, dual-pump machines employ two pumps for enhanced control and efficiency, allowing for better management of fluid exchange and toxin removal, which is beneficial for more complex clinical scenarios.</p></p>
