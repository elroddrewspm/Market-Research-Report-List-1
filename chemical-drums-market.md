<p><p>The chemical drum market comprises three main types: steel drums, plastic drums, and fibre drums. Steel drums are durable and suitable for hazardous materials, offering excellent protection against external conditions. Plastic drums are lightweight, resistant to corrosion, and ideal for storing chemicals that may react with metals. Fibre drums, made from compressed paper, are typically used for dry, less hazardous substances and provide a cost-effective option with good strength and lightweight characteristics. Each type serves specific storage and transportation needs in chemical handling.</p></p>
