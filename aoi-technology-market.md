<p><p>AOI Technology encompasses two primary market types: 2D AOI and 3D AOI. The 2D AOI market focuses on two-dimensional inspections, providing quick visual assessments of surface defects and solder joint quality on printed circuit boards. In contrast, the 3D AOI market utilizes advanced imaging techniques to assess component heights and volumetric data, allowing for more detailed inspections of complex assemblies. These technologies enhance manufacturing quality control by improving defect detection and ensuring product reliability in various industries.</p></p>
