Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) filters are widely used for their cost-effectiveness and compact size, offering good performance for lower frequency applications. In contrast, Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters deliver superior performance in higher frequency ranges, featuring advanced designs that minimize insertion loss and enable greater bandwidth. Both filter types are essential for optimizing mobile device functionality and enhancing receiver sensitivity in a competitive market.</p></p>
