<p><p>The Industrial Equipment Fastener Market is primarily divided into two types: threaded fasteners and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, such as bolts, screws, and nuts, feature helical ridges that allow for secure connections by engaging with corresponding threads. In contrast, non-threaded fasteners, including pins, clips, and rivets, rely on their shape and design to create stability and secure components without threads. Both types are essential for assembling machinery and equipment, ensuring reliability and structural integrity in various industrial applications.</p></p>
