<p><p>The stackable board-to-board connector market is divided into vertical and horizontal connection types. Vertical connections allow boards to be stacked directly on top of each other, optimizing space and height in compact designs. In contrast, horizontal connections facilitate lateral alignment, enabling boards to connect side-by-side. Both types serve various applications in electronics, providing essential interconnectivity for devices while addressing different spatial and design requirements. The choice between them depends on the specific layout and design constraints of the application.</p></p>
