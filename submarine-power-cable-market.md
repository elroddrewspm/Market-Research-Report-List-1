<p><strong>The Submarine Power Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Core Cable</li><li>Multi Core Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submarine power cable market primarily consists of single core and multi-core cables. Single core cables are typically used for high voltage direct current (HVDC) applications, allowing efficient long-distance electricity transmission with reduced losses. In contrast, multi-core cables contain multiple conductors, enabling the transfer of alternating current (AC) and offering flexibility in power distribution. Each type serves specific needs, with single core cables excelling in efficiency for extensive runs and multi-core cables providing versatility for various installations and configurations.</p></p>
