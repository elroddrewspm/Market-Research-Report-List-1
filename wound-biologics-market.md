<p><p>The wound biologics market encompasses three primary types: biological skin substitutes, enzyme preparations, and growth factors. Biological skin substitutes provide scaffolding for tissue regeneration and enhance healing in chronic wounds. Enzyme preparations facilitate the debridement of necrotic tissue, promoting a healthier wound healing environment. Growth factors are used to stimulate cellular proliferation and migration, accelerating tissue repair. Together, these products play a critical role in improving wound care outcomes and supporting the body's natural healing processes.</p></p>
