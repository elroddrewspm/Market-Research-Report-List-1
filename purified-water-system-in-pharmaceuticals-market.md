<p><strong>The Purified Water System in Pharmaceuticals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange System</li><li>RO System</li><li>Distillation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Purified water systems in the pharmaceuticals market include three primary types: Ion Exchange Systems, Reverse Osmosis (RO) Systems, and Distillation Systems. Ion Exchange Systems effectively remove ionic impurities, ensuring high purity. RO Systems utilize semi-permeable membranes to eliminate contaminants, achieving a high level of purification. Distillation Systems involve boiling water to separate impurities, ensuring the water meets stringent quality standards. Each system plays a crucial role in producing purified water, which is essential for pharmaceutical manufacturing and compliance with regulatory requirements.</p></p>
