<p><p>Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) are specialized equipment used for excavating tunnels in various geological conditions. The market is primarily divided into two types: Soft Ground TBMs and Hard Rock TBMs. Soft Ground TBMs are designed for softer, less stable soils and often use techniques like earth pressure balance to manage the surrounding environment. In contrast, Hard Rock TBMs are built for tougher, more rigid rock formations, employing powerful cutting tools to efficiently slice through solid ground, facilitating infrastructure development projects.</p></p>
