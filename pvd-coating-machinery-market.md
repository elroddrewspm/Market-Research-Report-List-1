<p><p>PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition) coating machinery market encompasses various types of equipment used for applying thin coatings. Evaporation equipment utilizes thermal energy to vaporize materials, allowing them to deposit on substrates for enhanced properties. Sputtering equipment relies on ion bombardment to eject particles from a target material, achieving high precision coatings. Other equipment includes techniques like laser deposition and chemical vapor deposition, which also serve specialized applications. Each type helps industries improve durability, aesthetics, and functionality of surfaces.</p></p>
