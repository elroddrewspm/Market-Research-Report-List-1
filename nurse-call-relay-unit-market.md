<p><p>The Nurse Call Relay Unit market comprises two primary types: Hard-Wired and Wireless Nurse Call Relay Units. Hard-Wired Nurse Call Relay Units utilize physical cabling to connect patient call buttons to the nursing station, ensuring reliable communication, especially in settings where power outages are a concern. Conversely, Wireless Nurse Call Relay Units operate using radio signals, offering greater flexibility in installation and reducing maintenance costs. Both types enhance patient safety and improve response times in healthcare facilities.</p></p>
