<p><ul><li>Bypass Feeder Type</li><li>Output Circuit Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Arc-fault Protection Device market includes two primary types: Bypass Feeder Type and Output Circuit Type. The Bypass Feeder Type is designed to ensure that power continues to flow during maintenance or fault conditions, allowing for uninterrupted service. In contrast, the Output Circuit Type focuses on protecting individual circuits from arc faults, enhancing safety by disconnecting power when an arc fault is detected, thereby reducing the risk of electrical fires and equipment damage. Both types play crucial roles in modern electrical safety systems.</p></p>
